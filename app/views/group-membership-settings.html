Membership Settings

Select Membership Control

Enter Subgroups

Group your members according to their role/status(Donors, Officers, etc.). You can also set the visiblity of the subgroup to the members. This is optional and can be changed at any time.

Visible - members can see that they are assigned to this subgroup

Invisible - members cannot see that they are assigned to this subgroup
